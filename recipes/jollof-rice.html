Nigerian Jollof

The Life of every Nigerian party!

Nigerian jollof rice, or "party jollof" is a one pot, tomato and pepper infused rice with a layer of crispy rice at the bottom.It is ever present at any Nigerian ceremony or event. This recipe aims to show you how easy and exciting it is to prepare a pot of steaming Nigerian Jollof

Ingredients

First you will need:

  • 5 medium sized Roma tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, roughly chopped
  • 1 medium sized onion
  • Tomato paste
  • 2 cups of parboiled rice
  • 2 cups of chicken stock
  • Salt to taste
  • Thyme
  • Seasoning
  • Water as needed

Preparation

Now lets get cooking!

  1. Blend your tomatoes, red peppers in a blender for about 45 seconds
  2. Heat your oil in a medium sized pot on medium heat
  3. Fry the mixture of tomato paste, blended pepper an tomatoes, and chopped onions for about 30 mins
  4. Turn down the heat and add the chicken stock and other seasonings
  5. Add the parboiled rice to the pot and mix it very well with the tomato sauce
  6. Add water so that the rice will soften, cover the pot and leave to cook for thirty mins
  7. When the liqud has almost dried up, add the remaining tomato sauce, cover and let it cook for another 5 to 10 mins
  8. Turn of the pot, mix thoroughly and your Jollof Rice is ready to be consumed!
